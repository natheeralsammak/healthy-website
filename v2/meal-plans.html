🔑 Keys to Meal Plan Success

Whichever diet you follow, these principles maximise results.

Prep

Batch Cook Weekly

Cook proteins, grains and veg in bulk on Sundays so healthy meals take under 5 minutes to assemble.

Hydration

Drink 2–3L Water Daily

Hunger is often thirst. Start every meal with a full glass of water to reduce overeating.

Consistency

80/20 Rule

Eat on-plan 80% of the time and allow flexibility for 20%. Perfection is the enemy of progress.
