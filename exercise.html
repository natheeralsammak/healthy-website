Workout Plans

Science-backed training programmes designed specifically for your body and goals.

Men's Training Overview

Build strength, burn fat and boost testosterone with compound lifts and high-intensity cardio.

Strength

Compound Lifts First

Squats, deadlifts, bench press and rows form the backbone of every session — maximum muscle, maximum burn.

Cardio

HIIT for Fat Loss

2–3 HIIT sessions per week elevate your metabolism for up to 24 hours after training ends.

Recovery

Rest & Grow

Muscle is built during recovery. Sleep 7–9 hours and take active rest days to prevent overtraining.

Women's Training Overview

Tone, sculpt and strengthen with programmes designed to work with your hormones and energy levels.

Sculpt

Lower Body & Glutes Focus

Hip thrusts, squats and lunges activate the glutes and build lean muscle where women see the most impact.

Cardio

Low-Impact Fat Burn

Cycling, swimming and dance cardio protect your joints while torching calories — perfect for all levels.

Mind & Body

Yoga & Flexibility

Weekly yoga improves posture, reduces cortisol, and complements strength training for a balanced body.
